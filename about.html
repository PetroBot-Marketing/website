Founded in Dec 2019, PetroBot Technologies is an Indian deep technology startup providing innovative robotic NDT solutions using indigenously developed remotely operated Robotic crawlers. PetroBot is DPIIT, Govt. of India-recognized startup company under Startup-India policy and an MSME registered startup. PetroBot was founded by IIT alums and oil & gas industry experts with vast experience in various robotic NDT projects.

Our team has worked in the Inspection and Maintenance of the Oil and Gas Industry, and we know how challenging it can be to open and clean tanks, inspecting at height by setting up scaffolding and in highly gaseous environment. That's why we're dedicated to providing safe, efficient and affordable solutions that make inspection easier.

We are committed to satisfying the customers by ensuring internationally accepted accuracy and on-time execution of robotic inspection services and continually improving the effectiveness of our inspection services. We are committed to providing a safe working environment meeting all applicable statutory and regulatory requirements.
