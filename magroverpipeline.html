PetroBot MagRover for Pipelines

Waterproof| PESO Certified | Flyer

Introducing MagRover for Pipelines, a new product from the MagRover lineup. This innovative robot is designed to inspect pipelines with an OD of 10 inches and above, providing a massive amount of information on their health compared to Manual UT Grid Scan (Spot Reading).

One of the key features of the MagRover is its custom-engineered wheels, which provide superior adhesion to the pipeline. This allows the robot to navigate even the most challenging surfaces, ensuring a thorough inspection. The MagRover is also highly specialized, with a platform that adjusts to different outer/inner diameters of the pipeline.

Safety is always a top priority at PetroBot , as it is PESO Certified for use in hazardous areas. This means that it can be deployed in a wide range of environments, providing peace of mind for operators and asset owners. The robot can be controlled from a safe distance away, eliminating the need for scaffolding and other potentially dangerous environment for humans.

In addition to its safety features, the MagRover for Pipelines is also highly advanced. It is capable of taking both UT spot thickness readings and UT scans, providing a comprehensive view of the boiler tube's condition. This allows asset owners and operators to make informed decisions about maintenance and repair.

Overall, the MagRover for Pipelines is a valuable service for asset owners and operators. It provides a safe, efficient, and comprehensive solution for inspecting pipelines, ensuring that they are in good condition and ready to operate at peak efficiency.
