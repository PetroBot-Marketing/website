PetroBot MagRover for Shell & Roof

Waterproof| PESO Certified | Flyer

Introducing MagRover for Shell & Roof, the first robot from our MagRover Lineup. This innovative robot is designed to inspect an asset's external shell and roof, providing a massive amount of data using UT Scanning to make better decisions compared to sparse and inconclusive UT spot reading.

One of the key features of MagRover is its custom-engineered wheels, which provide better adhesion to the asset shell, allowing the robot to navigate even the most challenging surfaces. In case of obstructions, such as wind girders, the MagRover can easily steer its way around them to ensure a thorough inspection.

Safety is always a top priority, and the MagRover for Shell & Roof is PESO Certified & ATEX Complaint for use in hazardous areas. This means that it can be deployed in a wide range of environments, providing peace of mind for operators and asset owners alike.

In addition to its safety features, the MagRover is also highly versatile. It can inspect assets of any diameter and height, providing a comprehensive view of the condition of the shell and roof. The robot can be controlled from a safe distance away, eliminating the need for scaffolding and other potentially dangerous equipment.

Finally, the MagRover is capable of taking both UT spot thickness readings and UT scans, providing a comprehensive view of the asset's condition. With the ability to capture a vast amount of data, the MagRover is a valuable tool for making informed decisions about asset maintenance and repair.

Overall, the MagRover for Shell & Roof is a revolutionary service that provides a safe, efficient, and comprehensive solution for inspecting the external shell and roof of any asset.
